# Changelog

All notable changes to the CUFinder Go SDK will be documented in this file.

## 1.0.0 (October 11, 2025)

All API services implemented, as below:

#### Company Services
- **CUF** - Company URL Finder: Get company domain from company name
- **LCUF** - LinkedIn Company URL Finder: Get LinkedIn URL from company name
- **DTC** - Domain to Company: Get company name from domain
- **DTE** - Domain to Emails: Get company emails from domain
- **NTP** - Name to Phones: Get company phones from company name
- **FCL** - Find Company Lookalikes: Get similar companies
- **ELF** - Enrich LinkedIn Fundraising: Get company fundraising information
- **CAR** - Company Annual Revenue: Get company revenue data
- **FCC** - Find Company Children: Get company subsidiaries
- **FTS** - Find Tech Stack: Get company technology stack
- **ENC** - Company Enrichment: Enrich company information
- **CEC** - Company Employee Countries: Get employee countries
- **CLO** - Company Locations: Get company locations

#### Person Services
- **EPP** - Enrich Profile: Enrich LinkedIn profile information
- **REL** - Reverse Email Lookup: Get person and company info from email
- **FWE** - Find Work Email: Get email from LinkedIn profile
- **TEP** - Person Enrichment: Enrich person information with full name and company

#### Search Services
- **CSE** - Company Search: Search for companies with advanced filters
- **PSE** - Person Search: Search for people with advanced filters
- **LBS** - Local Business Search: Search for local businesses
